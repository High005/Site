About High 0.05

It all started with debates with University Professors where bottles were uncorked when the P-value of the analysis in progress was less than 0.05. At that moment smiles blossomed and above all we began to share, as the English say, the “High five” with researchers of every background and specialty from biologists to Medical Doctors. To dedicate even an additional dose to that magical number, the whole company declared his love by adopting the name of so loved P = 0.05.

Technology is just a tool that can become useful if it brings large-scale changes to everyday life and can identify concrete and high-impact fields of application.
